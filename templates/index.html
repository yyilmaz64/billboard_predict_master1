Directions
  • Use the search bar below to choose a song to run through our algorithm! It will predict whether this song will be 'a hit' next week, as defined as appearing on the Billboard Top 100 chart the following week.

  • The autofill text comes from songs in our Billboard database...but if your song doesn't appear in the lockdown, never fear! Type in the name of the song, and information on it will be retrieved from Spotify!

Behind the webpage:
  • Underlying this prediction is a linear regression algorithm
  • It was trained on Spotify song characteristics as seen on our Top Tracks Playspace data page, as well as weekly Billboard history data from 2018-2021.
  • Unexpectedly, we discovered that the Spotify song characteristics didn't have much to do with the song success.
  • In retrospect, it seems obvious that the strongest predictor of a song appearing on the Billboard charts next week...was appearing on it this week.
